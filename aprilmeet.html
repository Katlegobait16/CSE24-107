April Meet Recap

Published: April 25, 2025

The April meet was an absolute blast! Over 150 cars showed up for April’s massive Gaborone night cruise, turning the city streets into a vibrant display of automotive passion. From classic muscle cars to custom-built tuners, the variety was incredible.

The cruise started at the Gaborone CBD Parking Deck and wound its way through the city, drawing crowds and excitement at every turn. The roar of engines and the glow of custom lights created an unforgettable atmosphere. Attendees enjoyed mingling, sharing their builds, and connecting with fellow enthusiasts.

A special shoutout to all who participated and helped make this event a resounding success. We look forward to seeing even more of you at our next event! Stay tuned for photos and videos from the meet, which will be posted soon on our social media channels.
