Summer Slam 2025 - Recap

Date: (Past Event) Held earlier in 2025

Location: National Stadium Parking Lot

Summer Slam 2025 was an absolute triumph! We witnessed a massive turnout with over 200 cars gracing the National Stadium parking lot. From stunning custom builds to roaring muscle cars and sleek imports, the diversity of vehicles was incredible. It was a fantastic day filled with revs, camaraderie, and a shared love for automobiles.

Highlights from Summer Slam:

  • Over 200 cars on display.
  • Live DJ and entertainment.
  • Food trucks and refreshment vendors.
  • "Best in Show" competition winners announced.
  • Community voting for "Fan Favorite" car.

Thank you to everyone who made Summer Slam 2025 an unforgettable event. We can't wait to see you at the next one!
