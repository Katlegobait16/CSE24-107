Build of the Month: Neo's Turbocharged GTI

Published: May 15, 2025

For our May "Build of the Month," we are thrilled to feature Neo’s incredible turbocharged GTR Skyline! This car is a true testament to dedication and meticulous craftsmanship, boasting a custom turbo setup that pushes its performance to exhilarating new levels.

Neo has put countless hours into perfecting this build, from engine modifications to suspension tuning and aesthetic enhancements. The result is a car that not only looks stunning but also delivers an unforgettable driving experience on both the street and the track.

We caught up with Neo to get the full scoop on her build, the challenges she faced, and her favorite aspects of the car. Check out our upcoming feature article for an in-depth look at this magnificent machine, complete with detailed photos and specs!
