Track Day Showdown 2025

Date: June 10th, 2025

Location: Francistown Raceway

Get ready to push your limits at the Francistown Raceway! Our annual Track Day Showdown is back, offering a thrilling opportunity for drivers to test their skills and their cars on a professional track. This event caters to various experience levels, from first-timers to seasoned track veterans. Safety briefings and professional guidance will be provided.

What to Expect:

  • Timed hot laps.
  • Drift exhibition zone.
  • Professional driving instructors available.
  • Food and beverage stalls.
  • Spectator friendly environment.

Pre-registration is highly recommended to secure your spot!
