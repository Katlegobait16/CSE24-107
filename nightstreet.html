Night Street Meet 2025

Date: May 18th, 2025

Location: Gaborone CBD Parking Deck

Join us for an electrifying night of car culture at the Gaborone CBD Parking Deck! This meet is open to all car enthusiasts, whether you're bringing your highly modified tuner, a classic beauty, or just coming to appreciate the automotive art. Expect a lively atmosphere, good vibes, and plenty of photo opportunities.

What to Expect:

  • Showcase your customized vehicles.
  • Network with fellow car enthusiasts.
  • Opportunity to meet local car clubs.
  • Music and food vendors will be on site.

Don't miss out on the biggest night street meet of the year!
