Drift Session 2025 - Recap

Date: (Past Event) April 2025

Location: Molepolole Open Grounds

The smell of burning rubber filled the air at the Molepolole Open Grounds during our epic Drift Session in April 2025! Drivers showcased incredible control and style, leaving tire marks and awe-struck spectators in their wake. It was a day dedicated to the art of drifting, with both seasoned pros and aspiring drifters taking to the makeshift track.

Event Highlights:

  • Tandem drift demonstrations.
  • Open drift sessions for registered participants.
  • Drift clinic for beginners.
  • Spectacular tire shredding action.
  • Energetic crowd and supportive atmosphere.

We extend our gratitude to all the drivers and fans who joined us for a day of controlled chaos and high-octane fun!
